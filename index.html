How to download your Instagram data

Follow these steps to get your follower and following data from Instagram

    Request a download in HTML format

  3. 3

    Select Followers and Following from available data

  4. 4

    Wait for Instagram to email you the download link (usually within 48 hours)

    Download and extract the ZIP file
